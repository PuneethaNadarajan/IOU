Create a group
Managing your shared expenses with IOU starts with a group. You can add any number of participants,
who share various expenses in different forms, to the pot. Participants and expenses can be added, edited or deleted at any time.
After creating a group you will receive an email with a link to the group.
Send this link to all participants so they can access the group from anywhere
at any time and add expenses.
